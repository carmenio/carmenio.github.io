ABOUT

My name is Christopher Armenio and I love to take photos. In my spare time I like to scavenge around places to find unique and quirky shots to take as well as play sports. I have a diploma in PDM, Postmodern Design, and I am currently working on a new film project with the theme of love. I have been working in this field for 3 years this year and I wish to lead the future of PDM.

LOCATION

Most of my photos come from around NSW in Sydney but I occasionally leave the state to take new and more moving and stimulating photos in places like the US or Korea.
